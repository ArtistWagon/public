Features

Your Band

Create an individually managed account that is linked to your band.

Split Transactions

Split profits and debts with other members in the band.

Request Money

Request money from other band members before making a purchase.

Withdraw Money

Connect an external bank account and transfer money back and forth free of charge.

Create IOU's

Generate an IOU when purchasing something for another band member.
