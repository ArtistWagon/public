Features

Artist Wagon is loaded with features for YOU the artist to help take the pain out of banking.

Your Bands

Be apart of multiple bands and manage each of them seperately.

Debit Card

For each band you are apart of, you have the option of obtaining a debit card that works at thousands of ATM's across the country.

Split Transactions

Split Profits and Debts among members in the band.

Request Money

Request money from the band before making a purchase.

Withdraw Money

Connect an external Bank Account and transfer money back and forth free of charge.

Create IOU's

Buy something for another member of the band. No worries! Genereate an IOU, set a deadline and wait for them to pay you back.
